Bayesian Uncertainty for BEV Object Detection

  • Developed fast Bayesian approximation for multi-sensor (LiDAR/camera) fusion for post-hoc (pre-trained models) and instance-level predictive uncertainty quantification
  • We build on top of Bayesian Neural Networks and improve the prediction step for high-dimensional outputs (e.g., multi-object detection), outperforming sampling/gradient-based approaches in speed and fidelity,
  • The solution is designed as a feature to be added to Huawei ADS sensor-fusion perception model and optimized with Huawei proprietary ADS datasets, with 15-20% increase in AvU score (Accuracy vs. Uncertainty) over legacy estimators, and better reliability in OOD scenarios (patent application in preparation)

Distributional shift detection and sensor cross-validation

  • Developed an OOD scenario detection system for autonomous driving systems (ADS) vision stacks using self-supervised learning (SIMCLR-based online anomaly detection) and sensor cross-validation system for fusion reliability monitoring, validated on ROS-CARLA simulation environments, in collaboration with external research partners.
